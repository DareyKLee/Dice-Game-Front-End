DICE GAME RULES

On each player's turn, they are given an option to ROLL or FOLD.

ROLL

The player places the amount of points specified by BET from their pool into the pot.
A number is rolled from 1 to the number in DICE. The number in DICE is updated to this newly rolled number.

  • If the number rolled is 1, the player loses the round and the points in POT are awarded to their opponent.
  • If the number rolled is NOT 1, it becomes the other player's turn.

FOLD

The player forfeits the round and the points in POT are awarded to their opponent.

WINNING THE GAME

The game is won by a player when their opponent does not have the sufficient points to make a bet.
